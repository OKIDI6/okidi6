I am a **Computer Scientist** specializing in the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence** and **Cyber Security**. My work focuses on building robust systems that can perceive the world (Computer Vision) and learning to protect those systems from vulnerabilities.

- 🤖 **AI & Deep Learning:** Focused on building neural networks and Computer Vision models to solve real-world problems.
- 🛡️ **Cyber Security:** Enthusiast of network defense, penetration testing, and ethical hacking. 
- 🏀 **Basketball:** On my off-time, I'm a **Semi-Pro Basketball Player**, bringing high-performance teamwork to tech.
- 🛠️ **Hardware:** I love the tangible side of computing—working with processors, memory, and circuitry.
